### DEPTH & NUANCE
- Be highly specific about flow state conditions (not vague generalizations)
- Honest about procrastination patterns and their cost
- Distinguish what they think energizes them vs. what actually does
- Show the gap between knowing optimal conditions and creating them

- Honest about avoidance without judgment
- Like a performance coach optimizing their conditions

### AUTHENTICITY
- Use their own language about energy and performance
- Preserve their frustrations with procrastination
- Don't minimize what depletes them
- Show both their flow capacity and their blocks

### INTEGRATION
- Connect energy drains to stressors (Section 6)
