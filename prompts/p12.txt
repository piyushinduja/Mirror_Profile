## ANALYTICAL FRAMEWORK

### MAJOR ARCHETYPES TO CONSIDER

**The King/Queen:**
- Leadership, sovereignty, order, responsibility
- Light: Benevolent ruler, wise leader, creates prosperity
- Shadow: Tyrant, controlling, demanding absolute loyalty, exploitative

**The Warrior:**
- Discipline, courage, protection, mission-focus
- Light: Defender, disciplined achiever, protector of values
- Shadow: Aggressor, ruthless, destroys without building, perpetual conflict

**The Magician/Visionary:**
- Transformation, vision, innovation, possibility
- Light: Change agent, visionary, alchemist of ideas
- Shadow: Manipulator, disconnected from reality, uses others for experiments

**The Lover:**
- Connection, passion, beauty, intimacy
- Light: Deeply connected, passionate, appreciates beauty and meaning
- Shadow: Addictive, boundary-less, loses self in others, jealous

**The Creator/Artist:**
- Expression, innovation, building, manifestation
- Light: Brings vision to reality, creates beauty and value
- Shadow: Perfectionist, never satisfied, self-absorbed, fears mediocrity

**The Sage/Mentor:**
- Wisdom, teaching, understanding, truth-seeking
- Light: Wise guide, shares knowledge, helps others grow
- Shadow: Detached, superior, judges others, paralyzed by analysis

**The Rebel/Revolutionary:**
- Change, disruption, freedom, breaking rules
- Light: Liberator, innovator, challenges stagnant systems
- Shadow: Destructive, oppositional for its own sake, burns bridges

**The Caregiver/Healer:**
- Nurturing, supporting, healing, service
- Light: Compassionate supporter, helps others thrive
- Shadow: Martyr, enables dysfunction, self-sacrificing to depletion

**The Explorer/Adventurer:**
- Discovery, freedom, adventure, new experiences
- Light: Courageous explorer, embraces unknown, growth-seeking
- Shadow: Perpetual wanderer, commitment-phobic, escapist

**The Innocent/Idealist:**
- Optimism, faith, purity, trust
- Light: Hopeful, sees possibility, maintains faith
- Shadow: Naive, in denial, refuses to see darkness, victim mentality

**The Jester/Trickster:**
- Play, humor, disruption, truth-telling through humor
- Light: Brings joy, points out absurdity, doesn't take life too seriously
- Shadow: Avoids depth, dismissive, uses humor to deflect pain

**The Orphan/Everyman:**
- Belonging, equality, realism, connection
- Light: Empathetic, realistic, values community
- Shadow: Victim mentality, cynical, expects abandonment
